Privacy Policy

Last updated: 3 July 2026 · Abask Travel LLC, Samarkand, Uzbekistan

What we collect

When you send us a tour inquiry, we collect the details you provide directly: your name, email address, preferred route and travel month, and anything you write in your message. If you later book a trip, we'll also hold the information needed to arrange it — passport details for permits and hotel registration, and payment information handled by our payment provider (see below).

How we use it

  • To reply to your inquiry and prepare a quote or itinerary
  • To arrange your trip — hotels, guides, transport, and permits — if you book with us
  • To send you information directly related to a trip you've booked or asked about
  • We do not sell your personal information to third parties

Payment information

Abask Travel does not store your full card details. Payments are processed by our payment or booking platform provider, who handle card data under their own security standards (typically PCI-DSS compliant). Once that provider is chosen, its name and privacy policy will be linked from here.

Third parties & tools this site may use

As the site grows, some of your data may pass through tools we use to run the business, such as:

  • A content management system, to store and publish tour information
  • A booking or payment platform, to process reservations and deposits
  • Website analytics, to understand which pages visitors find useful (aggregated, not sold)

This policy will list each provider by name once they're chosen.

How long we keep it

We keep inquiry and booking details for as long as needed to deliver your trip and meet our accounting and legal obligations, and delete or anonymize it afterward unless you ask us to keep in touch for future trips.

Your rights

You can ask us at any time what information we hold about you, ask us to correct it, or ask us to delete it (where we're not required to keep it for legal reasons). Contact us using the details below.

Contact

Questions about this policy, or requests about your data, can be sent to abasktravel@yahoo.com.
