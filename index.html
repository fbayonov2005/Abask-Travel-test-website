Why Travel With Abask

We don't run tours. We open doors.

  • Small groups, capped at 12 travellers per departure.
  • Guides are never subcontracted — every leader works for Abask, year-round.
  • Home-hosted dinners with real Silk Road families, not staged "cultural shows."
  • 24/7 on-the-ground support from our Samarkand office.

Inclusions, Actually Included

No nickel-and-diming

Unlike other operators who charge extra for every excursion, our fare includes all accommodation, most meals, all group transport, and a local expert leader — start to finish.

Meet the crew

What's included

  • Twin/double hotel accommodation with breakfast
  • Full-board meals as noted on each itinerary (cafés, restaurants and private houses)
  • Transport with all transfers for the whole tour
  • An English-speaking guide throughout
  • Entrance tickets to every sight on the itinerary
  • A daily bottle of water per person
  • Portage, where available
  • A welcome gift from our Samarkand souvenir store

What's not

  • International flights to and from Uzbekistan
  • Tips for guides, drivers and hotel staff
  • Soda, coffee and alcoholic drinks
  • Professional photo or video recording at historic sites
  • Travel and medical insurance
