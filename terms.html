Last updated: 3 July 2026 · Abask Travel LLC, Samarkand, Uzbekistan

1. Who these terms cover

These Terms & Conditions govern every tour, itinerary, and travel service booked with Abask Travel LLC ("Abask Travel," "we," "us"), whether arranged by phone, email, or through abasktravel.com. Booking a tour, paying a deposit, or otherwise confirming a trip means you accept these terms on behalf of yourself and everyone in your travel party.

2. Bookings & confirmation

Every Abask Travel itinerary is private and tailor-made. A booking is only confirmed once we've agreed your itinerary, price, and travel dates in writing, and received your deposit. Prices quoted are per person unless stated otherwise, and vary by group size, hotel category, and season, as reflected in your written quote.

3. Deposits & payment

  • A non-refundable deposit of 20% of the total tour price is due at booking to reserve your dates, guides, and vehicles.
  • The remaining balance is due no later than 30 days before departure, unless otherwise agreed in writing.
  • Bookings made within 30 days of departure require full payment at the time of booking.
  • Accepted payment methods are listed at checkout and may include international card payment and bank transfer.

4. Changes by you

We understand plans change. Ask us as early as possible — we'll always try to accommodate a change of date or itinerary where suppliers allow it. Changes requested within 30 days of departure may carry supplier fees, which we'll disclose before confirming any change.

5. Changes or cancellation by us

On rare occasions we may need to change or cancel a tour due to circumstances beyond our control (regional safety advisories, natural events, government restrictions, or insufficient minimum numbers on a group departure). Where this happens, we'll offer an alternative date, an alternative itinerary of equivalent value, or a full refund of amounts paid for the cancelled portion.

6. Your responsibilities

You're responsible for holding a valid passport (with at least six months' validity), any visas required for Uzbekistan, Tajikistan, or other countries on your itinerary, and appropriate travel and medical insurance for the duration of your trip. We're happy to advise, but visa and entry requirements are ultimately your responsibility to confirm with the relevant embassy or consulate.

7. Liability

Abask Travel arranges accommodation, transport, guiding, and activities on your behalf through local and international suppliers. We select these suppliers carefully, but we are not liable for events outside our reasonable control, including delays, weather, or the acts of third-party suppliers, airlines, or authorities.

8. Cancellations & refunds

See our separate Cancellation & Refund Policy for the full schedule of what's refundable and when.
