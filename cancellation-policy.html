Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last updated: 3 July 2026 · Abask Travel LLC, Samarkand, Uzbekistan

Deposit

A 20% deposit is required to confirm any booking. The deposit itself is non-refundable once paid — it covers the guides, vehicles, and hotel space we reserve on your behalf as soon as your trip is confirmed.

If you need to cancel

Refunds on the balance you've paid (beyond the deposit) depend on how far ahead of departure you cancel:

Notice before departureRefund of balance paid
60+ days100% (minus deposit)
30–59 days50%
14–29 days25%
0–13 days0% — no refund

For example: cancelling 7 days before departure falls in the 0–13-day window, so the deposit and any balance already paid are not refundable at that point.

If we need to cancel

If Abask Travel cancels your trip (rather than you), you'll receive a full refund of everything paid, or the option to rebook a future date at no additional cost — your choice.

Changes of date

Moving your travel dates is treated separately from a cancellation. Ask as early as possible — we'll waive our own fees for a date change made 30+ days before departure, though supplier fees (hotels, trains, internal flights) may still apply and will be disclosed before you confirm the change.

Travel insurance

Because deposits and late cancellations are non-refundable, we strongly recommend independent travel insurance that covers trip cancellation, for exactly the situations this policy doesn't.

Contact

To cancel, change, or ask about a booking, contact us at abasktravel@yahoo.com or +998 66 235 0098 as early as possible — the date we receive your written notice is what the schedule above is measured against.
